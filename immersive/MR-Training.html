Under the Hood | SBS Transit MR Volvo Engine Training

Mixed Reality training module that simulates Volvo engine diagnostics and maintenance — helping SBS Transit technicians gain hands-on experience without disassembling a single real-world component.

Customer: SBS Transit || Organization: EON Reality

Summary

Maintaining high-performance engines demands not just skill, but precision. And for SBS Transit’s growing fleet of Volvo buses, training needed to scale—quickly, effectively, and with no room for error.

Our Mixed Reality training solution bridges the gap between physical workshop learning and digital precision guidance. Using MR headsets, technicians step into a hybrid world where real-world tools and virtual engine overlays work in perfect sync.

Trainees interact with:

  • A full-scale Volvo engine replica, virtually overlaid in 3D onto real space
  • Step-by-step procedures for diagnostics, part replacements, fluid checks, and error code interpretation
  • Hand-tracking and spatial guidance, allowing natural interaction with bolts, belts, filters, and control systems
  • Instant feedback and correction prompts to reduce guesswork and reinforce accuracy

Developed in collaboration with EON Reality, the module was optimized for:

  • Realistic engine behavior simulation
  • Instructor or self-paced training flows
  • Performance tracking for both individuals and teams

This isn't just simulation — it's transformation. By enabling live, instructor-led VR training, we unlock a scalable, safe, and engaging way to upskill teams no matter where they are.
