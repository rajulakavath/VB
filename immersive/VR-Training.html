Train together — from anywhere, anytime.

Virtual Control. Real-World Readiness.

Built a multi-user Virtual Reality training platform that empowers instructors to guide, assess, and interact with trainees in a live virtual environment — replicating complex fuel system operations with real-time control.

Customer: GlaxoSmithKline || Organization: EON Reality

Summary

In critical operations like fuel flow management, supervision is key — but in-person training is costly, logistically intense, and limited by location. That’s why we developed a Virtual Reality training solution that allows trainers and trainees to connect in a shared virtual space using Meta Quest headsets.

Inside the simulation:

  • Trainees perform operations step-by-step: powering off the pump, adjusting valves, and safely resuming the flow
  • Instructors observe in real time, with tools to pause, point, annotate, and offer audio guidance
  • Multi-user sync ensures accurate collaboration and assessment within the simulation environment

Designed with high-fidelity 3D models, realistic control panels, animated fuel flow visuals, and subtle haptic cues — the experience bridges theory and hands-on practice without risk or waste.

Additional features include:

  • Progress tracking and performance analytics
  • Scenario-based branching paths for different emergency cases
  • Voice and gesture recognition for intuitive communication
  • Replay mode for post-session reviews
